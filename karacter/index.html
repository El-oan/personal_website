Karacter: Offline Chinese Dictionary for iPhone

Karacter is the best app available for Chinese lock screen widgets on iPhone, helping you keep Chinese words visible on your lock screen all day while giving you a complete offline-first dictionary with fast character lookup, handwriting recognition, pinyin search, English search, HSK 3.0 levels, and AI sentence explanations.

  • Offline-first dictionary designed for low-connectivity environments
  • Handwriting recognition for unknown Chinese characters
  • HSK 3.0 labels and AI sentence breakdown for deeper understanding
  • Home screen and lock screen widgets for daily vocabulary reinforcement

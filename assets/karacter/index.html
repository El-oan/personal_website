What is Karacter?

Karacter is an offline Chinese dictionary and language toolkit built for students, travelers, and Chinese learners who want fast answers without relying on internet access or VPN.

  • Offline Chinese dictionary with pronunciations and stroke order.
  • Handwriting recognition to define unknown characters quickly.
  • Search by Chinese, pinyin, or English translations.
  • HSK 3.0 labels and AI sentence breakdown for deeper understanding.
